Civil engineering(CIVIL)

Civil engineers are the most highly sought for building the global future. The present Civil Engineering practices in our country have certain defined targets and are aimed to:

  1. provide water supply for irrigation and drinking water needs;
  2. develop the minor, medium and major irrigation systems;
  3. provide the community housing and to investigate, design and manage the construction of multi-storey buildings;
  4. design a new road, free way or tunnel, bridges and manage their construction;
  5. provide various alternatives to relieve traffic congestion and to solve transport problems;
  6. mange the maintenance of the large bridges that link most cities;
  7. develop new ways of treating and reusing storm water and waste water to preserve precious resources;
  8. prevent contamination of soil and groundwater from industrial activities;
  9. design systems to control erosion in rivers and protect people from the devastation floods;
  10. develop the concept of a GREEN BUILDING that produces more electricity than it consumes and has a self-sufficient water supply;
  11. draw up innovative foundation systems for unstable soils;
  12. mitigate the natural disasters;
  13. design, plan and execute the environment friendly drainage and sewerage projects and
  14. work with a consortium to design eco-tourism lacustrine projects.
